The Geography of Homelessness

Why and How New York City Places Homeless Shelters

By Laura Karpas

October 28, 2025 — New York City

New York City stands at the epicenter of a nationwide crisis of affordable housing and an epidemic of homelessness. In recent years, the city experienced the highest levels of homelessness since the Great Depression of the 1930s. In August 2025, over one hundred thousand people slept each night in NYC shelters, and thousands more slept unsheltered on the streets of New York.

Over the years, New York City has implemented a series of laws and approaches that govern why and where homeless shelters are placed:
