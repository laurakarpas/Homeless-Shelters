The Geography of New York City Homeless Shelters

By Laura Karpas

October 28, 2025 — New York City

New York City stands at the epicenter of a nationwide crisis of affordable housing and an epidemic of homelessness. The United States is experiencing the highest levels of homelessness since the Great Depression of the 1930s. In August 2025, over one hundred thousand people slept each night in NYC shelters, and thousands more slept unsheltered on the streets of New York. The majority of the shelter population is families, with one third of that number being homeless children.
